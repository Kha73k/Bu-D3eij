Questions

Is it really private?▾

Yes. Every conversion and AI tool runs locally on your PC. Your files are never uploaded anywhere.

Is it free?▾

Completely. It's free and open-source under the MIT license — no ads, no subscriptions, no limits.

Do I need Python, ffmpeg or a GPU?▾

No. The installer sets up everything it needs. A GPU makes the AI tools faster, but they fall back to CPU automatically.

Is my data sent anywhere?▾

Never. The app works fully offline after the AI models download once. Nothing about your files leaves the device.
