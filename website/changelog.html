VERSION HISTORY

Changelog

From a simple file converter to a full local AI toolkit — here's how Bu D3eij grew.

v4.3Marquee grows again

Image → Prompt & ASCII Art

Turn any image into a detailed text-to-image prompt, or render it as ASCII art — both local, both free.

v4.2New section

Nexus — everyday utilities

Offline currency, unit & time-zone converters, plus a QR-code generator — no ads, no sign-ins.

v4.1GPU upgrade

Sharper image AI

The upscaler moved to UltraSharp V2 and the background remover gained a BiRefNet-HR tier that keeps individual hair strands.

v4.0New section

Sonara — audio stem splitter

Split songs into vocals, drums, bass & other with a real-time 4-stem mixer. The app's first GPU-accelerated tool.

v3.2Vanguard expands

Text extraction & font ID

OCR every line out of a screenshot, or identify the font in an image — added alongside the AI text detector.

v3.0New section

Vanguard — AI text detector

Estimate how likely a piece of text is AI-generated, with sentence-level highlighting — running entirely on-device.

v2.0New section

Marquee — image editing

The first AI image tool: a background remover that exports a clean transparent PNG. The upscaler followed soon after.

v1.0The beginning

The file converter

Where it started: drag-and-drop conversion for documents, images, audio and video, with a Recent-history store.
